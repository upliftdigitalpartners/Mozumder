Mozumder Transport Agency

Established in 2008, Mozumder Transport provides core land transportation across the country. The pool offers various types of vehicles — Prime Mover 10-wheeler, 20-feet container, 20-feet trailer, 30-ton, and two variants of 40-feet trailer (4-axle and 5-axle).
