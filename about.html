About Mozumder

Seventeen years of disciplined execution, earned one shipment at a time.

Mozumder is a well-established logistics and transportation enterprise headquartered in Chattogram — serving industrial and corporate clients across Bangladesh with a diversified fleet, nationwide reach and an unwavering commitment to safety, efficiency and reliability.

Our Story

From a transport service to a diversified logistics enterprise.

Mozumder is headquartered at K.R. Complex (3rd Floor), Gate No-09, Block-K, PC Road, Halishahar, Chattogram. The company began its journey as a transport service provider and has successfully earned the trust of over 100 major industrial clients across Bangladesh.

Our client roster includes Marico Bangladesh Limited, Berger Paints Bangladesh Limited, Arla Foods Bangladesh Limited, Akij Bashir Group, DBL Group, Nasir Group, Noman Group and many others.

With strong operational capabilities, Mozumder specializes in handling a wide variety of cargo — from light commercial goods to exceptionally heavy industrial equipment. We operate a diversified fleet consisting of trucks, covered vans and our own prime movers, enabling seamless nationwide transportation with high efficiency and reliability.

Our Vision & Mission

The principles that drive every decision we make.

Vision

The premier global logistics partner.

At Mozumder, our vision is to be the premier global logistics partner known for setting the industry standard in innovation, reliability and sustainability. We aspire to seamlessly connect the world through efficient and environmentally responsible logistics solutions — fostering prosperity for our clients, employees, and communities.

Mission

Exceptional logistics, tailored to your needs.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional logistics services tailored to the unique needs of our clients, while consistently upholding the highest standards of safety and sustainability. We are dedicated to client-centric excellence, innovation and technology, environmental responsibility, empowering our team, global connectivity, ethical and responsible business practices and adaptability.

At Mozumder, we are driven by these principles, and we strive to make a meaningful impact on the world of logistics. Our commitment to excellence, innovation and sustainability guides us as we work tirelessly to be the logistics partner of choice for businesses worldwide.
